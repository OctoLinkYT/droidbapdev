Connect to your Droid

This web app uses WebBluetooth to communicate with your Droid. Make sure you have a Droid from Droid Depot, turned on and in range of your computer.
When using this web app, your Droid should not be connected to its remote control or the Droid Depot app. It's safe to reboot your Droid before trying to pair it.
